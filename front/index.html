Chirp Model Showcase

Welcome to the Chirp Model Showcase!

This is an interactive demonstration of Google's Chirp model for speech-to-text.

Navigate to the Singing Contest tab to try it out.

The Chirp Singing Contest

You have to sing precisely on top of lyrics to earn points (accuracy, pronunciation and timing).

Press 'Start Singing' and the microphone is yours! Good Luck!
